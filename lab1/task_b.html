P is a tag for new paragraph. Paragraphs are usually represented in visual media as blocks of text separated from adjacent blocks by blank lines and/or first-line indentation, but HTML paragraphs can be any structural grouping of related content, such as images or form fields.

Div element has no special meaning at all. It represents its children. IT can be used with class, lang and title attributes to mark up semantics common to a group of consecutive elements.
